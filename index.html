    Game Rule

    There are two types of games in this game: maze and music.

    In maze levels, use arrow keys to move the character, answer the question to get out of the maze.

    In music levels, tap Space to turn the line to the rythme to avoid obstacles.

    Collect diamonds to get a better grade. Note you will be awarded with the number of diamonds only when the first time you pass the level, just like you take courses in college! But of course, you can always try previous levels again and again just for fun.

    Press Enter to start the game!
