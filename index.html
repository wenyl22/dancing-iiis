    Welcome to the Adventure of Knowledge and Music

    Embark on a journey filled with challenges and rewards in our dual-mode game, where you'll navigate through mazes and keep the rhythm in music levels. Here's how to master each mode:

    Maze Levels

    Navigate with Arrow Keys : guide your character through the labyrinthine paths.

    Solve Questions: Each maze holds 4 questions. Answer it correctly to collect the diamond attached to it.

    Music Levels

    Feel the Beat: Tap the Space bar in sync with the rhythm to maneuver the line and avoid obstacles. Timing is key!

    Stay on Track: Keep your focus and rhythm to navigate through the level successfully. The more precise you are, higher the probability of earning a diamond.

    Rewards and Progress

    Diamonds Await: Collect diamonds scattered throughout the levels to earn a better grade. You will be awarded the diamonds you've collected only the first time you pass a level, just like taking a course in college.

    Revisit and Refine: Feel free to replay previous levels anytime. Although it's irrelavant to your score, the adventure is yours to enjoy.
